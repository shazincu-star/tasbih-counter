About Our Website

"Remember Allah" is a simple Islamic website created to help Muslims remember Allah daily through Quran, Adhkar, and useful Islamic tools.

🌟 Vision

To build a peaceful digital place where Muslims around the world can remember Allah, strengthen their faith, and stay connected to Islam every day.

🎯 Mission

To provide useful Islamic tools such as Quran Surahs, Adhkar, Tasbih counter, Qibla compass, and the 99 Names of Allah for easy daily worship.

💎 Our Values

Faith, sincerity, simplicity, and spreading beneficial Islamic knowledge that helps people remember Allah more in their daily life.
